New York City Data Wranglers

WELCOME October 2014

We're a group of people who enjoy working with data. We're open to scientists, statisticians, software engineers, hackers, analysts and techies. We gather once a month at our coding Meetup, each time focusing on a different dataset to analyze in teams, pairs or individually. We also organize presentations of interesting results of techniques in R, Python, and Hadoop. Whenever possible, we work with commercial data that isn't available outside the group.
